Research & Publications

IRJET Publication • 2026

Sahara Bot – An Elderly Care Management System

Published in the International Research Journal of Engineering and Technology (IRJET)

Read Paper

Sahara Bot is an AI-powered elderly care management system designed to help senior citizens live more independently through intelligent voice assistance, health management, and caregiver connectivity. The research explores how conversational AI, OCR-based prescription reading, reminder automation, emergency alert mechanisms, and AI-driven assistance can improve the safety, well-being, and quality of life of elderly users while reducing caregiver burden.

Research Contributions

  • ✔ OCR-based medicine and prescription recognition for improved medication management.
  • ✔ Automated medication reminders, hydration alerts, and daily routine scheduling.
  • ✔ Emergency voice detection with instant caregiver notifications and monitoring dashboard.
